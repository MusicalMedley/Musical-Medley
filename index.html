Jagannath Rath Yatra Bhajans - Divine Melodies

Immerse yourself in the spiritual essence of the Jagannath Rath Yatra with our sacred collection of enchanting Bhajan songs. Experience the divine fervor of Puri's grand chariot festival through these devotional melodies, celebrating Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra.

Whether you're looking for classic Odia bhajans, traditional chants, or modern renditions, this page offers a spiritual musical journey. These Rath Yatra music tracks are perfect for meditation, devotion, and celebrating this auspicious occasion globally.

Popular Rath Yatra Bhajan Collection

Jai Jagannath - Bhajan

A powerful chant dedicated to Lord Jagannath, echoing the spirit of the Yatra.

Bana Parila Mo Deha - Namita Agrawal

A classic Odia Bhajan, deeply revered during the Rath Yatra festival.

Kalia Kalia - Santanu Sahu

A popular modern Bhajan praising Lord Jagannath's divine form.

Jagannath Ho Kichhi Maguni Tate - Sikandar Alam

A timeless prayer to Lord Jagannath, expressing devotion.

Ahe Nila Saila - Bhikari Bal

An iconic Odia Bhajan, a staple during the Rath Yatra celebrations.

Jaga Hata Re Dori - Suresh Wadkar

A beautiful Bhajan signifying the devotion to Lord Jagannath.
